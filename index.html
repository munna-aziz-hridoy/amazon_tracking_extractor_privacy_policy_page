Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18/09/2024

Welcome to the privacy policy for the Amazon Order Tracking Extractor extension. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

Information We Collect

This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal information, tracking numbers, or other data. All information processed by the extension remains locally on your machine and is not transmitted or stored externally.

How We Use Information

The extension interacts with Amazon Seller Central to extract tracking numbers from orders. The extracted tracking numbers are temporarily stored in your browser's local storage only for the duration of your session. These numbers are displayed to you within the extension interface and can be copied to your clipboard for your personal use.

Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions to function properly:

  • Tabs: To open new tabs for accessing Amazon Seller Central order pages.
  • Scripting: To inject content scripts and extract tracking numbers from the order pages.
  • Storage: To temporarily store the extracted tracking numbers during your session.
  • Host Access: To access Amazon Seller Central order pages for tracking number extraction.

Data Security

We do not collect or transmit any data outside of your local environment. All interactions take place directly between the extension and Amazon Seller Central within your browser. Since no data is collected or stored, there are no concerns about data breaches or unauthorized data access.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date at the top.
